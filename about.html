studies

I have a 3 years degree in psychological sciences and techniques and took a 2 years specialization in psychology of social and workplace-related processes and of development (although I have a strong interest in clinical too). Still waiting to be able to start my intership, but truthfully I carry everything I study with me and end up having long debates with friends even about fictional characters and how a real person would not react the way they do - which brought me to write a thesis about ptsd out of annoyance towards how lightly it was treated in a tvshow I used to watch (and of course over my extreme interest in the topic).
Needless to say I read up far too much about every possible topic that hits me, though my favorite sources are always the people who share their journeys, giving a completely different point of view on therapy and society expectations that no book can offer.
